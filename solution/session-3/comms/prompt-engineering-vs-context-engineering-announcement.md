# Event Announcement: Prompt Engineering vs Context Engineering

**Subject:** Stop guessing with prompts — learn what actually works when building AI apps

---

You've tweaked your system prompt for the hundredth time and the model still hallucinates. Sound familiar?

**Prompt Engineering vs Context Engineering: What Actually Works** is an evening of talks for developers who are done with prompt voodoo and ready to learn systematic approaches to building reliable AI applications.

## What You'll Get

- **The Prompt Engineering Playbook** — Marcus Johnson (Senior Developer Advocate at CloudAI) breaks down the patterns that consistently work and the anti-patterns that waste your time
- **Context Engineering: Designing What the Model Sees** — Elena Vasquez (Product Manager at ToolChain AI) shows how structuring the right context matters more than crafting the perfect prompt
- **What Actually Works in Production** — Alex Rivera (Freelance AI Consultant) shares real-world war stories from shipping AI at startups
- **Panel: The Great Debate** — All three speakers go head-to-head on what matters most
