## Why This Variant Won
The workshop-lab format best matches the brief's goal: "attendees leave with a working RAG pipeline they can extend." The community-first variant (v1) had too much networking and not enough coding time. The deep-dive variant (v2) was too lecture-heavy for a workshop. The lab format strikes the right balance — short intros keep context fresh, and guided exercises ensure everyone builds something real.
