# Building Production RAG Pipelines — Deep Dive

**If you've built a basic RAG demo and wondered "how do I make this actually work in production?" — this is your event.** Three hours of dense, advanced content with two of the sharpest minds in the space.

## What's Happening

This is not a beginner workshop. Sarah Chen will live-code a production RAG pipeline from scratch, covering chunking strategies, embedding selection, and retrieval optimization. Then Priya Patel will go deep on evaluation metrics, reranking, hybrid search, and fine-tuned embeddings — the advanced techniques that separate demos from production systems.

Expect 130+ minutes of technical content with minimal breaks. Come ready to learn.
