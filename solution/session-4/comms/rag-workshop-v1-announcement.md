# Building Production RAG Pipelines — Community Edition

**New to RAG? You're in the right place.** This workshop is built for learning together — ice-breakers, group exercises, and mentors at every table. No one gets left behind.

## What's Happening

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is how you make LLMs actually useful with your own data. Instead of a lecture, we're doing this as a community — you'll pair up, build together, and learn from each other.

Sarah Chen will walk you through the architecture, Marcus Johnson will make sure the concepts click, and Elena Vasquez will show you why this matters for real users. Plus: snacks, networking, and a show-and-tell at the end.
